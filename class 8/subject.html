● General Instructions:-
i) Use A4 size activity (practical) papers for writing the answers.
ii) Use black and blue ink only. No other colour ink is allowed.
iii) Use pencil for drawing(if any).

Answer the following questions:
1. Write short note on the following:-
i) Pasteurization of milk ii) Law of conservation of mass iii) Conditions favouring a chemical change. iv) Characteristics of physical changes v) Characteristics of chemical changes vi) Rusting of iron.

i) Evaporation ii) Vaporization iii) Sublimation iv) Condensation v) Dissolution vi) Melting.
