Chemistry

● General Instructions:-
i) Use A4 size activity (practical) papers for writing the answers.
ii) Use black and blue ink only. No other colour ink is allowed.
iii) Use pencil for drawing(if any).

Answer the following questions:
1.Compare solid, liquid and gas on the basis of following properties:
i) Flow characteristics ii) Texture iii) Compressibility iv) Strength of inter molecular forces v) Inter molecular space vi) Position of particles.

2. Define the following terms:
i) Atoms ii) Molecules iii) Dissolution iv) Catalyst v) Rusting of iron

3. Differentiate between the following:
i) Evaporation and boiling ii) Physical and chemical changes.

4. In terms of the change of heat energy, chemical reactions can be classify in how many types? Give two examples of each type of such chemical reactions.


5. Draw the labelled diagram of the following:
i) Sublimation process ii) Distillation process iii) Separation of immiscible liquids using separating funnel.
